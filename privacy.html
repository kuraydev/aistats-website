Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

Short version: AI Stats is a fully on-device macOS app. We do not run any server that receives your data, we do not use analytics or tracking SDKs, and we do not collect, store, link, or share any personal information. Your credentials and usage history live on your Mac, not on ours.

1. Who we are

AI Stats ("the app") is developed and published by Coursion ("we", "us"). Contact: kuraydev.io@gmail.com.

2. What AI Stats does

AI Stats is a native macOS menu-bar utility that displays your own usage, limits, and spending across AI services you already have accounts with (e.g. Claude, ChatGPT, Codex, Cursor, Anthropic API, OpenAI API, Gemini).

3. Data we collect

None. AI Stats has no backend, no user account system, and no analytics. We do not receive or log any information about you, your device, your usage of the app, or your AI accounts.

4. Data stored on your Mac

The app stores the following locally on your device:

  • Credentials you enter when adding an account — API keys or provider session cookies — are stored in the macOS Keychain, encrypted by the operating system.
  • Usage snapshots (quotas, limits, spending numbers returned by each provider) are cached in a local SwiftData database inside the app's App Group container so that the app and its widget can read the same data.
  • Preferences such as notification thresholds, polling pause state, and appearance options are stored in the app's own UserDefaults.

None of this ever leaves your Mac.

5. Network traffic

The only network requests the app makes are directly from your Mac to the AI provider you chose, using credentials you entered for your own account (for example, calls to the Anthropic or OpenAI API, or fetches from the Claude / ChatGPT / Codex / Cursor web sessions you are signed into). AI Stats does not route this traffic through any intermediary.

6. Tracking, analytics, and advertising

We do not use analytics SDKs, crash reporters that collect personal data, advertising frameworks, tracking pixels, or fingerprinting. The app's privacy manifest (PrivacyInfo.xcprivacy) reflects this: NSPrivacyTracking = false and no collected data types are declared.

7. Children's privacy

AI Stats is not directed at children. Because we do not collect any data from any user, we do not knowingly collect data from children either.

8. Data deletion

To remove all AI Stats data from your Mac, delete any accounts from within the app (this removes Keychain entries) and then uninstall the application. No data remains with us because we never had any.

9. Third-party services

When you add an account, AI Stats communicates with the AI provider you selected. That provider's privacy policy governs their handling of your account. AI Stats itself does not share any data with third parties because it does not hold any.

10. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above and, for significant changes, note them in the app's release notes.

11. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email kuraydev.io@gmail.com.
